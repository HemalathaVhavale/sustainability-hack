Thinking Green for
Software Applications

Carbon emissions threaten every part of the planet. It’s a global problem that requires global cooperation.

Our mission is to create consensus around the carbon footprint, as well as provide a calculator for measuring the carbon emissions from software applications in order to make our planet greener.

Changing the world is possible. Let’s begin it today.


We can reduce Carbon FootPrint by following simple steps in our daily life.
   1.Use bicycles and electric cars.
   2.Turn off lights when natural light is sufficient.
   3.Unplug electronics in use.
   4.Use the stairs as often as possible.
   5.Switch to LED Bulbs

Ready to reduce Carbon Footprint in applications?

This is a movement we would like to begin. There are millions of software applications currently. If we can reduce the Carbon FootPrint in these applications and data centres, we could reduce the carbon footprint to a great measure. Try out our calculator, to decode the amount of carbon footprint used by your application and the steps to reduce it.

“Caring for our planet should be our fundamental duty and every possible step should be taken to reduce the carbon footprint”
