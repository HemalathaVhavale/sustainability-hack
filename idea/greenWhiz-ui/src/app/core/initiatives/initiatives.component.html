Carbon Zero


Achieving a net zero carbon footprint.


What is Carbon Zero? How Can this be achieved?

Carbon and other green house gases are released into air and act as a blanket, holding the heat in our atmosphere and warming the planet.

Carbon Zero just means the amount of anthropogenic emissions added and removal from the atmosphere are the same. So in this way we will reach an equilibrium, when the amount of carbon emissions we add is no more than the amount taken away. In this way, we can avoid global warming due to green house gases.
